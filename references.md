
1. **Donald E. Knuth, The Art of Computer Programming, Volume 2: Seminumerical Algorithms (3rd Edition).**
	- Classic reference for algorithms related to arithmetic, including large number operations and numerical methods.

2. **Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, Clifford Stein, Introduction to Algorithms (3rd Edition).**
	- Comprehensive coverage of arithmetic algorithms, binary search, and computational problem solving.

3. **Wikipedia: Arbitrary-precision arithmetic**
	- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arbitrary-precision_arithmetic
	- Overview and techniques for handling large numbers in programming.

4. **Wikipedia: Binary search algorithm**
	- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Binary_search_algorithm
	- Theory, implementation, and applications of binary search.

5. **NPTEL: Programming, Data Structures and Algorithms**
	- https://nptel.ac.in/courses/106/106/106106145/
	- Video lectures and notes on arithmetic algorithms and efficient computation.
